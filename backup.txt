                <h1>
                    Who is UC Open Data?
                </h1>
                <p>
                    Originally created in 2019, UC Open Data is <i>UChicago's first and only open data project.</i> We focus
                    on collecting, creating, analyzing, and sharing data/data products related to UChicago. More specifically, this
                    involves:
                    <ul>
                        <li>
                            <p >Scraping websites (ethically) to organize data into a readable format
                            </p>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            <p >Reaching out to representatives of student government / UChicago admin
                                to obtain data that otherwise wouldn't be accessible</p>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            <p >Pushing this data we obtain onto a website for members of our
                                community to use</p>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            <p >Analyzing and visualizing the data we obtain to understand the data we
                                find</p>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            <p >Writing journalistic articles to explain the data's story and reach
                                broader audiences</p>
                        </li>
                        <li>
                            <p >And much more!</p>
                        </li>
                    </ul>
                </p>
                <h1>
                    Want to join UC Open Data?
                </h1>
                <p>
                    Members pursue data science/data journalism projects individually or in small teams throughout the quarter. We generally meet once a week for two hours, with times varying each quarter depending on when is best for everyone in the group. During spring quarter 2024, we'll be meeting <i>Wednesdays from 5-6:30 PM in the IOP Living Room!</i>
                </p>
                <p>We accept new members with both technical and non-technical backgrounds and
                    provide an open environment to learn while working, with experienced group members always open to help. Subscribe to
                    our <a href="https://lists.uchicago.edu/web/info/ucopendata">listserv</a> for more info, and feel free to email
                    uchicagotechteam@gmail.com if you have any questions or would like to join!
